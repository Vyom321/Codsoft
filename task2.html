About the Event

The Badminton Bash 2024 is an exhilarating event where players of all skill levels come together to compete, learn, and enjoy the spirit of badminton. Our event features singles and doubles matches, professional coaching sessions, and fun activities for all attendees.

Event Schedule

  • Day 1: Opening Ceremony & Initial Rounds

    Kick-off the event with an exciting opening ceremony followed by the first rounds of matches.

  • Day 2: Semi-Finals & Coaching Clinics

    Watch the top players compete in the semi-finals and participate in professional coaching clinics.

  • Day 3: Finals & Award Ceremony

    The grand finale with the final matches and the award ceremony to honor the champions.
